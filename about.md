I am a Research Manager with DT Global, an international development firm based in Washington, DC. I use field research, data analysis, and maps to understand conflicts and evaluate aid projects. From 2015-2018 I helped run the website [Syria Direct](https://syriadirect.org/).  

Interests: Conflict analysis, local governance, humanitarian access, aid (in)effectiveness, Arabic, Surveys, Rstats, QGIS, Tableau, PowerBI
